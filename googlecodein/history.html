History Of Sri Lankan Army

Established as the Ceylon Army in 1949, it was renamed when Sri Lanka became a republic in 1972. ... From the 1980s to 2009 the army was engaged in the Sri Lankan Civil War. The professional head of the Sri Lanka Army is the Commander of the Army, currently Lieutenant General Shavendra Silva.
